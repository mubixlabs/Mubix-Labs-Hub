1. Purchase a Pro or Pro + AI plan at [mubixlabs.studio/products/seo-insight-engine/pricing](/products/seo-insight-engine/pricing)
2. Check your email for your license key
3. In VS Code, run `SEO: Enter License Key`
4. Paste your key and press Enter

Your key is stored securely in VS Code's encrypted secrets storage. It is never stored in plain text or in your workspace files.

To verify your license status at any time, run `SEO: Check License Status`.
